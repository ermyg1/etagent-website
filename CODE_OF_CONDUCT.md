# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep collaboration respectful,
professional, and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and
abusive behavior are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or demeaning comments.
- Harassment, threats, or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that prevents productive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when behavior does not meet this standard.

Report conduct concerns to conduct@etagent.io. This address will become active
when official communication channels are operational.
